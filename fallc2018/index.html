About These Classes

PHYS 120-01

Astronomy class that surveys observational astronomy, the solar system, stellar properties, understanding of galaxies, and the birth and fate of our universe

ITMD 361-01

Covers creation of web pages and sites with the use of HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, and graphical applications. Also covers the client and server architecture of the Internet and related web technologies. Also addresses creation and deployment of modern, standards-compliant web pages. This class also comes with great dad jokes

SOC 322-01

Focuses on how technologies shaped and are shaped by human experiences. Deals with how technologies intersect with sociological themes like social stratification, stability and change, social control, identity, and community. Has an emphasis on the relationship between technologies and their social consequences.

ITMO 456-2

Sets out to teach students on how to set up and configure and industry-standard open source operating system. Adresses command-line commands, filesystem, filesystem hierarchy standard, applications, shells, scripting, UI, desktops, and many more.

ITM 100-01

Introduces students the steps necessary to analyze a problem in IT and identify and define the computing requirements appropriate to its solution. Students learn to analyze the local and global impact of computing on individuals, organization, and society.
